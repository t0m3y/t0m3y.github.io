Performances

SCOT performs at 4 citys each year, with 5 performances at each location. We are proud to be touring at New York City, Boston, Chicago, and Los Angeles for the 2017 season!

The Tour

Students undergo a rigerous 1-week-only rehearsal process in New York City before performances begin in week 2 of the camp. Each week they travel to a different city across the United States and work with professionals within the industry to gain valuble knowledge and contacts.

Workshops

At each city, the students will take part in workshops with industry professionals and the mentors that travel with them throughout the touring process.

Additional Information

Please contact us if you would like to find out more information about this program.

Application

Applications will be open soon! Applicants must maintain a GPA of 3.0 to be accepted and continue participation in SCOT.

Cost

The cost of attending Summer Camp On Tour is $6,950 per student. Cost of attendance includes: food, travel, lodging, and tickets to see a professional production at each stop of their 5-week/4-city tour.

Scholarships

Scholarships are available and will be offered to accepted students following final call-backs. Scholarships will be offered at the discretion of the mentors.

Sponsorships

Grandparents, extended family members, and friends are invited to sponsor a student to attend by a donation in their name.

Dates

Rehearsals begin Monday, June 25 in New York City, with performances beginning in Chicago on July 6th. The final performance takes place in New York City on Sunday, July 30th. Full calender can be viewed here.
